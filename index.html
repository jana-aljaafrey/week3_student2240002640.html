Who are we

We are Icy Color — a bright, playful ice cream spot made for everyone
who loves sweet moments. We craft creamy scoops and refreshing flavors
inspired by color, fun, and creativity. From classic favorites to fruity specials
every bite is made to feel fresh, happy, and unforgettable.

At Icy Color, we believe ice cream is more than a dessert — it’s a little celebration.
Come for the flavors, stay for the vibe.
